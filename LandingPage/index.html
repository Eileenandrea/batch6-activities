Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get accepted into your course, even if I have no background in software engineering?
Yes. We have some students with no coding experience but are fast learners.
Can I apply without a college degree?
Of course! As long as you're 18 years old, you can apply.
What can I expect to be after Avion School?
A Software Engineer! You'll be able to work as a frontend, backend or full stack developer.
Am I allowed to apply to companies that are not part of Avion's Career Partners?
Definitely! We help you apply to companies outside our network of Career Partners.
Will you be issuing certificates after the course?
You'll graduate with a certificate of completion and the skills you need for tech.
